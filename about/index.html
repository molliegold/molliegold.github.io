Mollie Gold is an artist living and smiling in New York City.



She is studying Communication Design at The New School with hopes of becoming a professional freelancer. Her main focus is UX/UI design but she also enjoys creating graphics and illustrations.

She has a knowledge of HTML 5, CSS 3, jQuery, and Javascript, as well as Adobe Photoshop, InDesign, and Illustrator. Her other passions include photography, typography, and writing.

When she isn't behind her computer, Mollie is doing yoga, laughing, and awaiting your message.
